About Me

Michayal Mathew is an aerospace engineering student at Texas A&M University. In his free time, he plays Intramural basketball, participates in Hackathons (24 hour programming competitions) as well as Entrepreneurial Idea Pitch competitions.

He considers his creativity and public speaking abilities to be his greatest assets. He has been conducting AR/VR Research with Dr. Darren Hartl for the past year, and just returned from working as a VR Intern at NASA Goddard Space Flight Center.
